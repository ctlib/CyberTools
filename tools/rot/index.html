                How it works
                

                    

                        ROT13 rotates letters by 13 positions (A↔N, B↔O, …). It's its own inverse. ROT5 rotates digits (0–9) by 5. ROT18 = ROT13 + ROT5 (letters and digits).
                        ROT47 rotates all printable ASCII (33–126) by 47. ROT48 is a variant of ROT47.
                    

                    
AUTO DETECT tries all variants and shows results - useful for CTF challenges where you don't know which ROT was used.
